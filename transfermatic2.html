Transfer MATIC from Binance to Metamask

Easy Steps

  • Go to Fiat and Spot
  • Select MATIC coin and click withdraw
  • Launch MetaMask and copy the address
  • Paste the MM Address in Recipient's BNB Address
  • Security Verification
  • Confirmation for withdrawal in Binance

Go to Fiat and Spot

Log in to the Binance

Click Wallet in the upper right corner of the webpage.

Then, click Fiat and Spot.

Select MATIC and click withdraw

Select MATIC by using the search box given.

Once you have found MATIC, click withdraw.

If MATIC balance is 0.00 in the binance, then please Trade and bring to Fiat and Spot wallet

Launch MetaMask and copy the address

Ensure that the wallet is on the Polygon Mainnet.Polygon Mainnet Network Setup.

Copy the MM address by clicking on it.

Paste the MM Address in the Address Box

Paste the address you copied from MetaMask in the address field.

Select the Network address as MATIC Polygon. Enter the amount you wish to transfer and submit.

Note: You need to send a minimum of 10 MATIC.

Security Verification

Email and phone , but it could be a bit different based on your country.

Confirmation for withdrawal in Binance

After few minute, the transaction will appear in your MetaMask wallet.
