Transfer MATIC from Metamask to Binance

Easy Steps

  • Go to Fiat and Spot
  • Select MATIC and Copy the Deposit Address
  • Launch MetaMask
  • Paste the Address and Enter the amount of MATIC
  • Click Confirm
  • Check Activity & Binance

Go to Fiat and Spot

Log in to the Binance

Click Wallet in the upper right corner of the webpage.

Then, click Fiat and Spot.

Select MATIC and Copy the Deposit Address

Select MATIC by using the search box given.

Once you have found MATIC, click Deposit.

In the Deposit Crypto window, select the Network as Polygon.

Then, copy the deposit address shown below the Network field.

Launch MetaMask

Ensure that the wallet is on the Polygon Mainnet.Polygon Mainnet Network Setup.

Paste the Deposit Address and Enter the amount of MATIC

Click Send and see the gas fee estimation

Click Confirm
